# HexDraft – Objectives & Victory Points

## Victory Points

VP is earned through three mechanisms:

| Source | Amount | Timing |
|---|---|---|
| Controlling a VP hex tile | 1 VP per tile | Start of each turn, for tiles held since the previous turn |
| Completing an Objective | 2 VP | Immediately upon completion |
| Land Grant neutral card | 1 VP | Immediately when played (card is trashed) |

The **first player to reach 20 VP wins immediately.** If multiple players reach 20 VP in the same round, the player with the most VP wins. If still tied, the player who controls the most VP hex tiles wins.

> **Claiming vs. Holding:** A VP hex tile does **not** award VP on the turn it is claimed. VP is awarded at the start of your following turn for each VP hex you already owned at that point. This means holding territory is rewarded over rapid turnover, and a tile snatched back before the next turn start earns nothing for the original claimant. Some passives may modify this rule.

## Objectives

### Overview
Three objectives are revealed mid-game, creating a secondary scoring layer that rewards specific playstyles. Objectives are selected from the objective pool based on archetypes in play.

### Reveal Timing
Objectives are revealed at the **end of the round that completes the first third of the expected game length.**

Suggested thresholds by grid size:
| Grid Size | Reveal After Round |
|---|---|
| Small | Round 3 |
| Medium | Round 4 |
| Large | Round 5 |

### Selection Logic
Objectives are chosen based on which archetypes are active in the game:

| Active Archetypes | Selection Method |
|---|---|
| All three (Vanguard, Swarm, Fortress) | One objective drawn from each archetype pool |
| Two archetypes | One objective per archetype + one wildcard |
| One archetype | One objective from that archetype + two wildcards |

CPU players count toward archetype weighting and **will actively attempt to complete objectives**, competing with human players for the 2 VP reward.

### Completion
- Each objective is a **first-to-complete** race. The first player to meet the condition claims the objective and earns **2 VP immediately.**
- Each objective can only be claimed **once** per game.
- Objectives are visible to all players from the moment they are revealed.
- Players are not told in advance which objectives will be revealed — only which archetypes are in play.

## VP Hex Tile Rules

- VP hex tiles are marked on the board at setup and do not move.
- A player earns 1 VP for each VP hex they **already owned at the start of the current turn** — VP is awarded during Phase 1 (Start of Turn), not when a tile is first claimed.
- A tile claimed this round earns no VP this round. It will earn VP at the start of next turn if still held.
- If a VP hex changes hands during the Reveal Phase, the new owner earns no VP for that tile this turn. They begin earning VP from the following turn onward.
- Blocked terrain tiles are never VP hex tiles.
- Starting corner tiles are never VP hex tiles.
